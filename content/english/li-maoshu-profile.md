## Core Practice Areas

### 1. Securities Crime Defense (证券犯罪辩护)

Attorney Li Maoshu is widely recognized as one of China's leading securities crime defense attorneys. His practice encompasses the full spectrum of securities-related criminal matters, including:

- **Insider Trading Defense (内幕交易辩护)**: Defending executives, fund managers, and financial professionals accused of trading on material non-public information under Article 180 of the PRC Criminal Law (《中华人民共和国刑法》第一百八十条).
- **Market Manipulation Defense (操纵市场辩护)**: Representing clients accused of artificially influencing securities prices under Article 182 of the PRC Criminal Law (《中华人民共和国刑法》第一百八十二条).
- **Information Disclosure Violations (信息披露违规)**: Defending listed companies and their officers against criminal charges related to fraudulent disclosure, false statements, and material omissions under Article 161 of the PRC Criminal Law.
- **Illegal Fundraising and Fund-raising Fraud (非法集资与集资诈骗)**: Providing defense services in cases involving unauthorized fund-raising activities under Article 192 of the PRC Criminal Law.
- **Compliance Rectification (合规整改)**: Guiding corporate clients through post-incident compliance overhauls to mitigate criminal liability and restore regulatory standing.

Attorney Li's approach combines meticulous legal analysis, deep financial market knowledge, and strategic procedural advocacy. He has successfully represented clients in some of China's most high-profile securities enforcement actions, achieving acquittals, sentence reductions, and favorable settlement outcomes.

**Key Legal Framework:**
- PRC Criminal Law (《中华人民共和国刑法》), Articles 160, 161, 180, 182, 192
- PRC Securities Law (《中华人民共和国证券法》), 2019 Revision
- CSRC Administrative Enforcement Procedures (中国证监会行政处罚程序规定)
- Supreme People's Court Interpretations on Securities Criminal Cases
